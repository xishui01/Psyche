Psyche Angled Mask Application Brush

ABOUT:

The brush helps ensure a flawless application of any mud mask using synthetic bristles for long lasting texture.

It provides mess-free application so that you won't waste product or worry about possibly bacteria infection from your hands onto your face.

The angle of bristle help you to smudge mud evently on face.

DESCRIPTION:

How to clean it?

1.Run your brush bristles under lukewarm water. Make sure to keep the bristles pointing downwards, avoiding the area where the handle meets the head. The water can loosen the glue that holds the bristles together!

2.Dispense a small amount of baby shampoo into the palm. Swirl the brush bristles into the baby shampoo until it creates a rich lather.

3.After you feel you've worked out the dirt and oil that has been trapped in the bristles, rinse the brush head under lukewarm water again.

4.Squeeze the excess water out of the brush by pulling it in between your thumb and the side of your index finger. If the water that is released is dirty, go ahead and repeat the cleaning process again. Once the water runs clear, you can move on to the next step.

5.Use a cloth or a paper towel to dry off the bristles, reshaping them as you go. Lay your brushes flat to dry over night.
